!TOPIC 2271 InitCustom,QkRefChart
!NOINDEX
constructor 2269QkRefChart.InitCustom(X1, Y1, X2, Y2 : Byte;
                                  LeftTee, CrossBar, RightTee : Char;
                                  var Colors : ColorSet;
                                  Options : LongInt);

Initialize the quick reference chart with custom connectors, colors, and
window options.

X1, Y1, X2, and Y2 represent the dimensions of the inner window (not
including any border or shadows). LeftTee, CrossBar, and RightTee are the
characters to be used to draw the line that divides the column headers at
the top of the window from the remainder of the chart. Colors contains the
colors to be used to draw the window, and Options are the window options.

!TOPIC 2281 Options,QkRefChart
!NOINDEX
!NOSEARCH
The following options may be set for a quick reference chart:

!NOWRAP
qrMousePage      = $0001;
qrSuppressBlink  = $0002;
!WRAP

qrMousePage indicates that clicking on the up or down arrows of a scroll bar
scrolls the chart by a full page, rather than a single line. qrSuppressBlink
indicates that attribute blinking should be suppressed when displaying color
samples.

See also:  2275qrOptionsAreOn  2274qrOptionsOff  2273qrOptionsOn
;

!TOPIC 2287 Commands,QkRefChart
!NOINDEX
!NOSEARCH
The following commands are available in the quick reference chart:

ccUp          <Up>, <CtrlE>, <CtrlW>
!LINE
Scroll window up one row.

ccDown        <Down>, <CtrlX>, <CtrlZ>
!LINE
Scroll window down one row.

ccLeft        <Left>, <CtrlS>
!LINE
Scroll window left one column.

ccRight       <Right>, <CtrlD>
!LINE
Scroll window right one column.

ccHome        <Home>, <CtrlQ><S>
!LINE
Scroll window left to column 1.

ccEnd         <End>, <CtrlQ><D>
!LINE
Scroll window right to last column.

ccWordLeft    <CtrlLeft>, <CtrlA>
!LINE
Scroll window left 10 columns.

ccWordRight   <CtrlRight>, <CtrlF>
!LINE
Scroll window right 10 columns.

ccPageUp      <PgUp>, <CtrlR>
!LINE
Scroll window up one page.

ccPageDn      <PgDn>, <CtrlC>
!LINE
Scroll window down one page.

ccEndOfFile   <CtrlPgDn>, <CtrlQ><C>
!LINE
Scroll to end of chart.

ccTopOfFile   <CtrlPgUp>, <CtrlQ><R>
!LINE
Scroll to top of chart.

ccHelp        <F1>, <ClkBoth>
!LINE
Request help.

ccBlinkToggle <CtrlB>
!LINE
Toggle attribute blinking.

ccQuit        <CtrlBreak>, <Esc>, <ClkRight>
!LINE
Close window.
